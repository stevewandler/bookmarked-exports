The Reality

What Your Librarians Are Dealing With

Texas compliance requirements have made library management more complex than ever. These are the daily challenges your team faces.

01
🔍

One Book at a Time

Librarians research each title individually across scattered sources. There is no single place to find what is publicly known about a book — so every review starts from scratch.

02
📄

No Paper Trail

Decisions live in emails, spreadsheets, and memory. When a parent or board member asks how a book was evaluated, there is no documented, defensible record.

03
⚠️

Liability Without a System

SB 13 and SB 412 create board-level accountability for every title in your collection. Without a system of record, the risk sits with your district.
