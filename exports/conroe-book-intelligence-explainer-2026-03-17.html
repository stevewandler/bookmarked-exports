Understanding Book Intelligence

How Bookmarked provides consistent, defensible, and sourced intelligence on your book collection — and why that matters for your board.

Prepared for district decision-makers
What Bookmarked Does
Bookmarked is a Book Intelligence platform. We provide structured, sourced intelligence about books to help your district make informed collection decisions.

"We provide intelligence, not instructions. Data, not directives. Every piece of information is tied to a specific ISBN and available to every reviewer in your district — the same information, every time."

For each book in your catalog, Bookmarked does the research your staff would otherwise do one title at a time — scanning professional reviews, news coverage, age and grade-level assessments, and publicly available information — and brings it together in one place, tied to the exact ISBN your district is evaluating. Instead of five librarians independently searching the internet for the same book, everyone works from a shared, centralized source of information.

Why This Approach Matters
Six reasons Bookmarked's methodology produces better outcomes for your board and your community.
01

Consistency — One System, One Answer

Every AI chatbot is different — different training data, different models, different versions. Even the same tool with the same prompt doesn't guarantee the same answer twice. When five staff members each open their own ChatGPT account and type in a book title, they're not using one system — they're using five separate systems that happen to share a name. Your board needs one answer they can stand behind, not a dozen that contradict each other. Bookmarked gives every reviewer the same intelligence, in the same format, every time.

02

Transparency — We Show Where Our Information Comes From

General AI tools synthesize from training data that may be incomplete, outdated, or incorrect — especially for newer titles. They'll give a confident-sounding answer even when they have no real information. Bookmarked is transparent about what we know and where it came from. We aggregate publicly available information — professional reviews, news coverage, and content assessments — the same research your staff would do manually, centralized in one place. We don't fabricate information about a book, and we don't pretend to know more than we do.

03

Defensibility — A Documented Process

When your board asks how a book was reviewed, your staff needs a documented process — not a screenshot of a chatbot conversation. Bookmarked creates that documentation as a natural part of the workflow: sourced intelligence, reviewer notes, committee input, and a clear record of how decisions were made. This is the difference between a research tool and a compliance system.

04

ISBN Fidelity — The Right Book, Not Just A Book

Your board policy requires ISBN-based lookup for a reason: different editions of the same title can have different content. General AI tools don't distinguish between editions, ISBNs, or versions. Bookmarked ties every piece of intelligence to the specific ISBN your district is considering — the exact edition, the exact book.

05

Collaboration — One Shared Workspace

Right now, your staff is managing feedback across spreadsheets, email chains, and individual notes. Bookmarked replaces that with a shared workspace where librarians, review committees, and administrators can see the same information, add their professional input, and make decisions together — all in one system that keeps a complete record.

06

Institutional Memory — Intelligence That Stays

AI chatbot conversations disappear or get lost in individual accounts. Bookmarked creates a persistent record for your district. Librarian notes, committee decisions, board actions — all in one place. When staff changes happen, the knowledge stays. When a title comes up again next year, the history is already there.

Where We're Headed

Verified, Source-Level Intelligence

Bookmarked is building partnerships with publishers to create a secure environment where publishers provide book content directly. Our AI reads the actual book, identifies compliance-relevant content, and generates verified intelligence — while protecting the publisher's copyright and the author's work. This creates ground-truth intelligence that no general-purpose AI tool can provide, because publishers will never give their content to a chatbot. Districts get the most accurate, most complete book intelligence available anywhere.
