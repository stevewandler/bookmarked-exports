Content Scanner

Complete Book Intelligence, From the Source — the first project of The Bookmarked Council.

The Challenge Today

Secondhand Information, Firsthand Decisions

Librarians evaluating books piece together information from multiple sources — reviews, descriptions, internet searches. No single source covers everything, and none are tailored to your district's specific compliance requirements. The result: gaps that create risk.

What Content Scanner Does

AI-Powered, Publisher-Authorized Content Analysis

With explicit publisher permission, Content Scanner reads actual book content — cover to cover — and produces a complete, objective, structured content profile. It applies consistent criteria to every page, every time.

This means:
  • Districts get firsthand intelligence, not secondhand opinions
  • Consistent evaluation criteria applied to every book
  • Compliance-relevant content identified that reviewers may have missed
  • Complete content profiles that support defensible decisions
What Content Scanner is NOT

NOT a replacement for librarians — it gives them complete information for better decisions

NOT unauthorized — every book requires explicit publisher participation

NOT available today — being co-designed with input from The Bookmarked Council

How It Relates to Book Intelligence

Two Layers, One Platform

Book Intelligence
Available today, serving 150+ districts. Analyzes everything publicly known about a book — metadata, reviews, flags, challenge history.
Content Scanner
Coming soon. Adds a new layer: intelligence based on what's actually in the book, with publisher permission.

Book Intelligence tells you what the world says about a book. Content Scanner tells you what's actually in it.
