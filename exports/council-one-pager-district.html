About the Council

The Bookmarked Council is an invitation-only advisory group where publishers and school districts co-design the next generation of book intelligence. Your input directly shapes what gets built.

First Project: Content Scanner

Today, Book Intelligence tells districts everything publicly known about a book. Content Scanner goes further — with publisher permission, AI reads actual book content so your team has complete, firsthand intelligence. Complete picture. No more gaps.
