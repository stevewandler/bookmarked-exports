Workstream Deep Dives

Key deliverables and success criteria for each track.

Design Sessions

Steve (facilitator) + Natasha (publisher co-facilitator)
  • 6 co-design sessions in April (2 publisher, 2 district, 2 joint)
  • 3–5 publishers representing different segments
  • 3–5 districts with strong librarian relationships
  • Each session: 60–90 min, recorded with consent
  • Internal prep 3 days before, debrief same day after
  • Success: 80%+ attendance, 3+ surprise insights

Product & Engineering

Sarah (PO) + Raju (VP Eng)
  • Real-time capture of product implications during sessions
  • Mockup/concept prep between sessions
  • MVP spec: Absolute / Next Phase / Nice-to-Have
  • Prototype sprint start May 19
  • AI agent-driven build (June–July)
  • Success: spec grounded in partner input, not assumptions

Collateral & Communications

Steve + Natasha
  • Publisher + district invitation briefs (1-pagers)
  • Content Scanner Vision Overview (NDA-only)
  • Pre-session context docs (T-5 days)
  • 24-hour post-session summaries
  • Monthly "What you said → What we built" updates
  • All materials PROD-002/PROD-004 compliant

Legal & Compliance

Jan Gilboy
  • All materials reviewed for IP implications
  • NDA status confirmed for all publisher participants
  • Recording consent from all participants
  • Monitor feedback channels for legal flags
  • Pre-launch legal review before preview event
  • Red flag: any external leak triggers containment

Internal Operations

Steve + Patrick
  • Prep sessions before each co-design (60 min)
  • Same-day debriefs after each session (45 min)
  • Synthesis work sessions (May)
  • Feedback loop infrastructure setup
  • AI agent processing pipeline activation
  • Weekly feedback digests for Steve

Recruitment (Pre-Program)

Natasha (publishers) + Patrick (districts)
  • Publisher targets: Saddleback (confirmed), Cajun Kids, Lerner/Rosen, 1–2 TLA connections
  • District targets: engaged customers, active compliance challenges, post-testing availability
  • Personalized invitations (not templates)
  • 15-min scoping calls before formal commitment
  • All confirmations by April 1
  • Steve approves all external communications
