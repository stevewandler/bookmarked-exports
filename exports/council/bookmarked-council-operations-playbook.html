Last updated: March 23, 2026

1 What Is The Bookmarked Council?

The Bookmarked Council is an invitation-only advisory group where publishers and school districts co-design Content Scanner — the next major Bookmarked capability. We named it "The Council" to give the program identity and weight with our partners.

Members participate in structured co-design sessions, give feedback on early concepts, and help shape a product that works for both publishers and districts. Their input directly influences what we build.

6 Important Rules

  • All external communications go through Steve before sending. Claude will always draft and show — never send on its own.
  • "Book Intelligence" describes what is publicly known about books. Never say "what's in the book." Bookmarked does not read book contents (that's Content Scanner).
  • "Content Scanner" reads actual book content with publisher permission. This is what the Council is co-designing.
  • We never position Bookmarked as replacing librarians. We give them superpowers — more time, better tools, deeper insights.
  • Lead with empowerment and time savings, not compliance burden or fear-based framing.
  • Always say "co-design" when describing what Council members do. Never "focus group," "beta test," or "product feedback."
