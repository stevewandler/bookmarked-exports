F

Feedback Loop Architecture

Agentic first. Recorded feedback processed by AI, not manually compiled. Feedback routes to product decisions in under 48 hours.

🎙️Recording / Input
→
📝Transcription
→
🤖AI Extraction
→
🔀Routing
→
🎯Product Decision

Collection Channels

  • Bi-weekly video check-ins (recorded, transcribed)
  • Monthly structured surveys (Google Forms)
  • Shared Slack channel for ad hoc feedback
  • Direct conversations (Natasha/Patrick capture)
  • Prototype usage analytics (PostHog)

AI Extraction Targets

  • Feature requests → Jira tickets
  • Pain points → Product priority input
  • Validation signals → Confidence markers
  • Concerns → Risk register
  • Workflow insights → Spec refinement
  • Quotable moments → Messaging (with permission)

Routing Rules

  • Critical blocker → Steve + Sarah same day
  • Feature request → Sarah → Jira within 48 hrs
  • Usability concern → Next sprint planning
  • Strategic insight → Steve weekly review
  • Legal/compliance flag → Jan + Steve same day
Q

Quality Control Checkpoints

Built-in quality gates at every phase. Red flags trigger immediate escalation.

Pre-Program (Before Apr 1)

  • 3–5 publishers confirmed
  • 3–5 districts confirmed
  • All materials PROD-002/PROD-004 compliant
  • Legal clearance on vision docs
  • Recording consent from all participants

During Co-Design (April)

  • Capture template complete within 48 hrs per session
  • No silent participant dropouts
  • Cross-session synthesis after each pair
  • Emerging patterns documented

Synthesis (May)

  • MVP spec with Absolute / Next / Nice categories
  • All design partners reviewed + commented
  • Engineering feasibility confirmed
  • Timeline realistic per Raju

Red Flags — Stop & Escalate

  • Design partner drops out without explanation
  • Feedback consistently contradicts direction
  • Engineering slips more than 2 weeks
  • Legal concern about content/IP
  • Any external leak of Content Scanner details
