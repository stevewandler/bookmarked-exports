What This Is

Building the future of book intelligence — with you, not for you

The Bookmarked Council is an invitation-only advisory group where publishers and districts shape how schools discover, evaluate, and trust books. We're not asking you to test a product. We're asking you to help design it.

📚

For Publishers

Your books deserve to be found by the right schools. The Council ensures that the intelligence system represents your catalog completely, accurately, and fairly.

  • Shape how publisher context appears alongside assessments
  • Design the data submission process that works for you
  • Influence what market intelligence you receive back
  • Ensure your books reach districts that need them
🏫

For School Districts

You know what you need better than anyone. The Council puts your real workflows, compliance challenges, and student needs at the center of what gets built.

  • Define what a complete book intelligence report looks like
  • Shape how compliance mapping works for your state
  • Replace the patchwork of reviews with something better
  • Ensure the product fits your actual workflow
🔮

For Book Intelligence

The Council isn't a one-time project — it's the permanent advisory body that shapes every major capability Bookmarked builds. Content intelligence is first. What comes next is up to you.

  • Grounded in real needs, not assumptions
  • Both sides of the ecosystem in the same room
  • Continuous feedback shapes continuous improvement
  • A growing community of the industry's best minds
From the Founder

Why This Group Exists

I started Bookmarked because I believe every student deserves to find the right book — and every librarian deserves the tools to make that happen confidently. We've built intelligence that helps 150+ Texas districts understand what the world says about their books. Now we're building something bigger: intelligence based on what's actually in the book, with publisher permission, so districts never have to guess again.

But we can't build this alone. Publishers understand their books. Districts understand their students. We need both perspectives in the room — not as focus groups, but as co-designers. That's why The Bookmarked Council exists. You'll have a genuine seat at the table, a direct line to the team building this, and first access to everything we create together.
SW
Steve Wandler Founder, Bookmarked
