Quality Control

Five quality gates from recruitment through launch. Red flags trigger immediate escalation.

Quality Gates

  • Pre-Program Gate

    Before April 1

    Participants confirmed. Materials compliant. Legal cleared. Recording consent obtained.

  • Post-Sessions Gate

    April 30

    All session captures complete within 48 hours. Cross-session synthesis done. Emerging patterns documented.

  • Spec Lock Gate

    May 16

    MVP spec finalized with Absolute / Next / Nice categories. All partners reviewed. Engineering feasibility confirmed.

  • Go / No-Go Gate

    July 21–25

    Product reviewed against MVP spec. Partner satisfaction measured. Technical readiness confirmed. Legal clearance.

  • Launch Ready Gate

    July 28

    Preview event complete. Design partners have first access. Launch communications approved by Steve.

Red Flags — Stop and Escalate to Steve

⚠️ Design partner drops out without explanation ⚠️ Feedback consistently contradicts direction ⚠️ Engineering timeline slips more than 2 weeks ⚠️ Legal concern raised about content or IP ⚠️ Any external leak of Content Scanner details ⚠️ Partner satisfaction trending negative
